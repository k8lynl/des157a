Best practices for form design

October 14, 2024

Link to article

The article “Best practices for form design” by Salim Ansari discusses the various ways a designer can improve the user experience of forms. Many of the methods to improve form UX follows the simple guideline of “less is more”. This principle ultimately minimizes the user’s “cognitive load and visual noise,” preventing the user from being overwhelmed by too many steps or information. Another guideline is to make things as clear and labelled as possible at all times. This prevents any confusion on the user’s side, and assists them in completing the form faster with more clarity. From colors to placement of text to the phrasing of instructions and feedback, even the simplistic of design choices are crucial to crafting an easy experience for form users.

An example of a website form with excellent UX is Airtable.
